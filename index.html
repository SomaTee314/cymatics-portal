How to Use

  1. 01

    Sound & playback

    In the first column, choose an Audio Mode: Upload Track (choose an Audio file), Healing Presets (pick a preset), or Manual tone. Set Frequency with the slider or number box when that mode uses it. Use Play, Pause, and Stop; adjust Volume. For an uploaded track, scrub the timeline and watch the time label while it plays.

  2. 02

    Advanced visuals

    In the second column, pick an Audio-Visualiser Portal (Balanced, Mandelbrot, or Julia) and set Particle count with the slider or number box. Press H to show or hide Advanced Visual Controls. Drag that window by its header; tap ↺ to snap it back to its default spot.
