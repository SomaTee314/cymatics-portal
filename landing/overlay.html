How to Use

  1. 01

    Sound & playback

    Everything you hear runs from the first control column. Choose a mode (track, preset, or manual tone), set the frequency, and add a file when you are using a track. Use Play, Pause, and Stop, turn Volume to taste, and drag the track-position slider when a file is playing.

  2. 02

    Advanced visuals

    The second column is for the picture. Pick an Audio-Visualiser Portal and a particle count. Under that, open Advanced Visual Controls for colours and deeper tweaks. Press H to show or hide that panel. Drag a panel by its header, or tap ↺ to put it back where it started.

  3. 03

    Start & explore

    Press Play: the pattern rides your audio in real time. For fullscreen, use the control on the bar above the visual. Two rail icons appear—Sound & playback and Advanced visuals. Hover to preview, click to pin, × or Escape to close a panel. Escape again leaves fullscreen.
