How to Use

Choose a quality, tap Go, then read this page and tap Enter Portal when you are ready. The back arrow in the top corner takes you to the previous screen.

  1. 01

    Sound

    On the main screen, the left column is for sound. Pick an Audio Mode: your own file, a Healing preset, or a Manual tone. Use Play, Pause, and Stop. Turn Volume up or down. Change Frequency with the slider or number box when it appears. For your own files, drag along the timeline to move through the track.

  2. 02

    Visuals

    The right column is for the picture. Under Audio-Visualiser Portal, choose Particle cymatics, Mandelbrot fractal, or Julia fractal. Use Particle count to make the image lighter or heavier. Press H on the keyboard to show or hide Advanced Visual Controls. Drag that panel by its title bar; tap ↺ to put it back in the corner.

  3. 03

    Account & sessions

    Tap Sign in or Sign up in the top corner to create a free account. That unlocks the full set of frequencies and visual modes in the browser for this site. If a control is locked or your session ends, follow the message on the screen. Visit the Pricing page for paid plans with unlimited time and extra features.
